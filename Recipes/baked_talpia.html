Baked Talpia

Description

Classic and healthy baked talpia with hints of lemon!

Ingredients

  • 4 Oz Talapia
  • 2 tbs Butter
  • 1 Sliced Lemon
  • Lemon Pepper

Steps

  1. Pre Heat the oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Place talpia in a oven save baking dish, add lemon pepper and dot fish with butter.
  3. Add the sliced lemon
  4. Bake for 20 to 30 minutes until fish fakes easily
  5. Serve and enjoy!
