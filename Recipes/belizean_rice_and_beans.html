Belizean Rice & Beans

Description

Belizean rice and beans is by far one of my favorite dishes. My grandma would make this for our family daily and that coconut milk gives it its own flavor, so yummy!

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of dried kidney beans
  • 1 1/2 qz of water (more or less if needed)
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1/2 medium onion
  • 1/2 medium bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 tbs of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tea spoon of salt (sea salt if you prefer)
  • 1/4 ground black pepper
  • 1 cup of white jasmine rice
  • 3/4 cups of water

Steps

  1. In a large container wash and soak kidney beans overnight.
  2. Drain and transfer the beans to a large pot. Add 1 1/2 qz of water, cover and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and simmer for about a hour or until beans are tender then add your galic cloves. Remove from heat and stir in the bell pepper, salt (or sea salt), oil and black pepper.
  3. In a medium sauce pan cook and stir rice over medium to high heat for about 3 minutes. Add in your coconut milk, water, kidney beans and bring to a boil. Cover the rice and cook on low heat for 30 to 40 minutes or until rice is tender.
