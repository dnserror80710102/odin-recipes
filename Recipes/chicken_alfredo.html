Chicken Alfredo

Description

This simple chicken alfredo dish is one go to dishes for nights with the family. Spice it up with added veggies or lemon pepper for some extra kick.

Ingredients

  • 1 lbs of uncooked fettuccine or linguine
  • 2 lbs Chicken breast, cooked and sliced
  • 1 oz. Alfredo pasta sauce
  • 10 oz. Mixed vegtables
  • 3/4 cups of Milk

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil for 8 minutes or untill tender.
  2. In a large sauce pan, add sliced chicken breast, alfredo sauce, milk and frozen vegtables over medium to high heat. Cook and stir until chicken is is cooked and vegtables are tender.
  3. Serve over cooked noodles and enjoy!
